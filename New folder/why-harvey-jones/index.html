Why Harvey Jones?

We have been handbuilding kitchens for more than 35 years and this experience means that we know exactly what it takes to deliver furniture that our clients are proud to use and to have in their homes.

We craft our kitchens in three timeless, beautifully made designs: Shaker, Linear and Original. Each design is handcrafted in our Cambridgeshire workshop and is built to last using traditional construction techniques.   

Each design is highly versatile and can be customised and personalised to your exact needs and tastes. Our kitchens can be painted after installation in any colour you choose, which means you can choose the exact shade that is perfect for your new room or experiment with multiple colours. Equally, you can select your choice from an almost endless range of handles and knobs, allowing you to give your kitchen a truly personal touch. We also offer a huge variety of worksurface options, from granite and marble to composites and real wood, and each of our showrooms display a range of the latest appliances.

No matter which range you select, we offer an impressive range of thoughtful storage solutions to ensure that everything has a home in your new kitchen. From clever mechanisms to make the most use of awkward corner spaces to our much-loved handcrafted oak removable cutlery trays and spice drawers, we can guide you towards the most practical solutions.

Your kitchen will be individually and carefully planned by one of our experienced designers who will work closely with you throughout the project to ensure that your brief is met. When you are completely satisfied with the plans for the kitchen, it will be submitted to our workshop where it will be made to order by our skilled team of joiners and cabinet makers.

We now have 31 kitchen showrooms across the UK. Our showrooms are the starting point for your project as they allow you to experience the quality of our construction first-hand and to see our ranges and the variety of cabinetry options we offer. You can find your nearest showroom by using our showroom locator.
