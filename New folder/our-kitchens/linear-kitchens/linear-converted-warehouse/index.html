Linear Studio in a Converted Warehouse

This stunning kitchen design shows the minimalist, contemporary qualities of Linear off to the full. 

The limited palette of white and grey used in the paint colour and worksurface adds to the light, airy feel of the space, and the stepped worktop heights subtly create different zones within the open-plan room.

Key features:
  • Painted in Dulux Chiffon White 4
  • Recycled ‘Polar Cap’ worksurface on the island Wild Brasilica worktops
