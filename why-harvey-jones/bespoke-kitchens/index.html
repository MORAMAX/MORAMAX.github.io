Bespoke Kitchens

No two Kitchens are the same. Every kitchen is designed and made to order, unique in its specification and tailored to the exact requirements of its owners. It is this versatility that is the key to our success. Our design experts will work with you to create a totally unique bespoke kitchen design that, down to the final detail, perfectly fits your brief.

Starting from your wishlist, our talented designers will guide you through the often daunting array of choices and options available in the process of planning your kitchen, and make practical and inspiring suggestions about the best use of your space. Whether you are looking for the perfect kitchen for a period room, a more contemporary design, or a timeless, classic space, our furniture will be precisely tailored to your specification.

We are famous for our painted kitchens which arrive at your home having been expertly primed with two coats of hardwearing white undercoat. Then, after installation, they can be handpainted by our expert painters in the colour (or colours) of your choice. This gives you freedom to choose the exact colours that are right for the space, and to put your personal stamp on the room. You might even choose to incorporate a unique pattern, like the kitchen above.

What's more, your cabinets can be adorned with the handles or knobs of your choice, too. We source handles from across the world in a variety of shapes, sizes and materials which allows you to customise the look of your furniture. We also source and supply a huge variety of worksurfaces, from clean, simple composite stone and Corian to intricate, naturally-patterned granites. We display a wide range of handle and worktop samples in each of our showrooms.

But it is not just externally that we offer real versatility. Because we build all of our kitchens to order in our own Cambridgeshire workshop, we have complete control over manufacturing. This means that we can adjust the sizing of cupboards, altering them as necessary so that they exactly fit your space. We can also alter the internal specification and configuration of our cupboards, incorporating innovative storage mechanisms or designing bespoke storage solutions for specific items. This gives you freedom to work with our designers to create a truly individual bespoke kitchen, customised to suit your personal requirements.

Our designers are on-hand to help you make the most of the versatility of our furniture and to create a space that looks beautiful and functions perfectly. Most importantly, our kitchen designers will listen to what you want and what you must have, helping you to achieve your perfect kitchen.
