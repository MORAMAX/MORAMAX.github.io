Bournemouth Showroom Opening April 5th 2012

We're delighted to announce that we will soon be opening a new showroom in Bournemouth.

The showroom, our 24th, will showcase exciting examples of our Linear, Original and Shaker kitchens, as well as our famous pantry larder and a host of worksurfaces, appliances and handles.

We're currently completing the installation and painting of the furniture and will be opening on April 5th 2012.  We'd be delighted to show you around when we open, and if you'd like a brochure in the meantime, please click here.

Showroom address:
Kitchenware Plus Kitchens
57 Poole Road
Bournemouth
