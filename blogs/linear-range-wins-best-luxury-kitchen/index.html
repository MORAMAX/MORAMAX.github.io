Company News

Author: David Grenham

Posted on 18 Nov 2011

We are very proud to announce that our Linear kitchen has won the Gold Award for 'Best Luxury Kitchen' at the House Beautiful Awards 2011!

We received the award at the star-studded evening, held at the stunning Freemasons' Hall in central London.  The judges were impressed by the way the Linear range combined a clean, modern aesthetic with traditional joinery techniques.    

The awards issue of House Beautiful magazine, which will contain full details of the event and its winners, will be on sale in February.
