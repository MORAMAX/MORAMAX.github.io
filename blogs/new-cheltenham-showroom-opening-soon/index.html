New Cheltenham Showroom Opening Soon

We are delighted to announce that we will soon be opening a brand new showroom in Cheltenham.

The showroom, our 22nd outlet, is located in Montpellier, close to the beautiful Montpellier Gardens. We are currently finishing off the installation of a wonderful range of kitchen designs, and will be opening on December 1st, 2011.  

We would be delighted to show you around when we open or, if you would like to discuss your project with one of our designers in the meantime, please call 0800 389 6938.

Showroom address:
Kitchenware Plus Kitchens
4-5 The Courtyard
Montpellier Street
Cheltenham
Gloucestershire
GL50 1SR
