Company News

Author: David Grenham

Posted on 03 Sep 2013

Click to view article
Our famous Pantry Larder is one of our most iconic pieces of furniture.  With soft-close oak drawers and spice racks, useful removable trays, wine rack and helpful granite cold shelf, it's the perfect place to keep all your kitchen must-haves close at hand.  And, like all of our primed-for-painting furniture, it can be handpainted in any colour you choose.

For all clients placing their design deposit with us during September 2013, we are offering a Pantry Larder absolutely free.  Visit one of our showrooms to see the Pantry Larder first-hand, call now to discuss your project with one of our talented designers or to book your complimentary design consultation: 0800 389 6938.

Find out more about our Pantry Larder here.


Terms and Conditions:
  • Pantry Larder to be supplied as per picture
  • Including granite cold shelf, oak trays and spice racks (not including food and accessories)
  • Design deposit must be placed between September 1st-30th 2013
  • Minimum furniture order value to qualify is £16,677.00 ex. VAT
