We are pleased to announce that, due to exceptional client demand, we have extended our Summer Sale!

For all new clients that place their design deposits with us during August 2015 we are offering 25% OFF our entire range of handmade cabinetry.

This offer must end on August 31st, so to make the most of these great savings please visit one of our beautiful showrooms now. Alternatively, call 0800 389 6938 to discuss your project with one of our experienced designers or to book your complimentary design consultation. You can find your nearest showroom by using our 'Showroom Finder' tool here.

In the meantime, click here to request your free copy of our brochure. Or, if you’d like to arrange a complimentary design consultation with a designer at your local showroom, you can also click here.
