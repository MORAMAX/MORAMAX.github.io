Buy Now, Pay Later Must End March 31st

In order to offer our clients more options to finance their new kitchen, we provide an optional Buy Now, Pay Later plan.

This plan means that, after paying a 25% deposit to secure the production of your kitchen, you need pay nothing more until 12 months after your credit agreement is signed.

To take advantage of this great offer, simply place your design deposit with us by March 31st  2012. 

Please click the link on the left for full details, or visit one of our showrooms for more information.

The credit facility will be provided by Hitachi Capital (UK) PLC, trading as Hitachi Capital Consumer Finance, 2 Apex View, Leeds LS11 9BH.  This company is a leading provider of consumer credit, working with many major UK high street retailers, and you will be entering an agreement with them to collect the final balance on behalf on Kitchenware Plus Kitchens.
