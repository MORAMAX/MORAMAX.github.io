Classic Painted White Shaker Kitchen

This is a great example of our timeless Shaker kitchen furniture.  Open shelving has been planned into this island so that cookery books and other household items can be displayed and easily accessed. The oak used to top the island has been echoed in the shelving above the sink to tie the room together.

Key features:
  • Painted in Dulux Chiffon White 4
  • Honed black granite worksurfaces with upstands and oak island top
  • Glazed cupboards add to the spacious feel
