Welcome to our Cheltenham showroom.

Located in beautiful Montpellier, our Cheltenham showroom is ideally located for clients throughout Gloucestershire and the Cotswolds. 

If you would like a free copy of our brochure please click here, or call 0800 389 6938 to discuss your project with one of our designers and to hear a little more about the way we work.

Directions: 

By Car: Exit the M5 at Junction 11, towards Gloucester/Cheltenham/ A40.  At the roundabout take the 3rd exit, continuing towards and then joining the A40.  At Benhall roundabout take the second exit onto Gloucester Road/A40. After approximately 1.5 miles, turn left onto Montpellier Street, and our showroom is 500 yards down on the left hand side. Metered street parking is available.

By Public Transport: Exit Cheltenham Spa railway station onto Queens Road.  Continue to the end, and the road joins Lansdown Road.  Bear left, continuing on Lasdown Road then turn left onto Montpellier Street.  Our showroom is 500 yards down on the left hand side.
