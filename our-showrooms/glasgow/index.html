Glasgow

Welcome to our Glasgow showroom.

This beautiful showroom is located in the heart of Glasgow city centre, close to the Museum Of Modern Art and a short walk from Glasgow Central railway station.

We'd be delighted to show you around or, if you would like more information in the meantime, please request your free brochure here or call to discuss your project with one of our designers.

Directions:

From Glasgow Queen Street railway station: Exit the station on to North Hanover Street, then head south. Turn left on to George Street, then turn right on George Square. Continue on to South Frederick Street, then turn right on to Ingram Street. Our showroom is located on the left hand side after approximately 50 yards.
